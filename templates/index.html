This method generates a prediction of the future price points of the selected Cryptocurrency.

Test Accuracy Method

This method tests the accuracy of the model by making predictions for a subsection of data and comparing them to the data we have for the predicted time period.

Prediction Intervals

These denote the time between each predicted price point, thus additionally denoting the length of time the prediction is for.
